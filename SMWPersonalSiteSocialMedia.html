Regretting Action Is Better Than Regretting Inaction

To me, this means I must take a stand for Equality.
It is now 2020, and it is time for humanity to evolve for the benefit of all.

We must listen to each other, have empathy, and empower each other by lifting each other and rightfully representing minority groups; we can make a good impact on these issues. There are many issues, and they run deep, but we can make a positive impact together.
